Real and Approximated

No? Let's have a look at larger angles.

The sine model is a poor approximation for large angles!

def real_pendulum_ode(conditions, t, ℓ=20, g=980):
    ##current state of θ and ω is in the conditions list
    θ, ω = conditions
    
    dydt = [ω, -g/ℓ*θ]
    return dydt
